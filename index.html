    We are a Charitable Social Enterprise

    Pluto is a not for profit charitable social enterprise committed to delivering a better quality of fitness and leisure, libraries and performing arts facilities for everyone. We are committed to providing access to quality community facilities - and more - at a price everyone can afford. We already manage facilities whole over the country, and we are adding more all the time operating under our brand.
